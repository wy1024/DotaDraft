About the creator :D

I am a UW CSE graduate who is also a Dota enthusiast!! Played Dota since Dota 1 and have ~5.1k MMR. I would say this tool does help drafting a lot and gives me a better understand of our team's weaknesses even before the game starts.

It is especially useful in picking heroes in ranked games and tournaments.


What to expect next:

Dota Drafts is very useful because it analyzes every Dota game played in all different skill levels. However YOU are not just anyone! Each player has his or her best heroes.

In the future We look forward to adding steam login and provide you with a more customized recommendation. We can analyze your match history and take into consideration your strengths and weaknesses.
