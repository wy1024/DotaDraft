A drafting example:


TI5 Semi-finals game

In the Ti5 semi-finals game EG vs. LGD, both teams decided to pick their core first and build a team around it.



EG went for Leshrac and Clintz, while LGD got their Gryo and SF. Now here comes the question, what will you pick next to work well with your team, and counter the opponent team.

This is what Dota Drafts suggests. Looking at EG's lineup, they have Clock solo off-lane, Leshrac mid, and a trilane with Visage as support, Clintz as the main carry. Therefore they needed another strong lane support, preferrably with lockdown in teamfights.

In the drafting phase, these are already Banned.

  • Bounty-Hunter
  • Techies
  • Anti-Mage
Considering the Lane, there aren't good places for these to go:
  • Huskar
  • Pheonix
  • Tinker
  • Elder-Titan

That leaves us with Earth Spirit, Winter Wyvern and Zeus.

  • Earth Spirit is indeed a very good choice as it has disables and stuns, and strong magic burst for teamfights.
  • Zeus can do massive damage with Clock's initiation, and Thundergod's Wrath can prevent Earthshaker from blink echo slam.
  • EG went for the other suggested pick, Winter Wyvern. Winter Wyvern forms a very strong lineup with Clintz and Visage. Also his ultimate "Winter's Curse" provides the team with the ideal lockdown and strong teamfights.

A strong draft phase gave EG a head start on the game, which eventually lead to their victories.
